How To Sell Your Swipes

Freshman with extra swipes left at the end of the week? Upperclassman with a meal plan you're not going to finish? Fill out our seller's form and we'll connect you with people looking to buy individual swipes. All you have to do is commit to being in the same place (ideally near a dining hall) for any amount of time, and be willing to go swipe a buyer into that dining hall when they ask.

When you fill out the seller's form, Swipe Market will add an entry for you, indicating which dining halls you're willing to swipe people into, what time you're available, and how much you're charging. When a buyer is interested, they'll click a button and enter their information, then we'll automatically send an email connecting you to each other.

After we connect you to your buyer, Swipe Market is out of the picture. It's your responsibility as the seller to make sure you get paid, and to use common sense when meeting up with strangers. Happy selling!
